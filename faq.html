FAQ


Who will cut my hair?

A fully trained, experienced, professional Master Barber.


Where do you cut the hair? I am in a cubicle, and have no office.

We set up in a designated location by the employer (conference rooms, gyms and vacant offices). Each client comes to the designated room at the time they schedule.


How much does it cost? How do I pay?

Flat Pricing: $25 (including tax and tip). Payable via CC through our website.


How long does it take?

You will be in and out in 20 minutes.


What if I am a few minutes late?

Please try to be on time or even early to your appointment. If you are more than 10 minutes late, we will not be able to service you at your pre-scheduled appointment time. Our appointments are booked 30 minutes apart so we need to stay on schedule for the next customer.


Do I get a shampoo?

No. We massage the scalp then apply a water spritz. We ask that you come to work that day with clean hair using little product.


Every time I get my haircut, I always have little hairs down my neck. How do you keep these off of me, so I can go right back to work without itching?

We totally understand. We use a double cutting cape and we always finish with a talc duster, so you look and feel fresh.


What about hygiene? Is it clean?

Our barbers always clean and disinfect their hands and tools with sanitizing gel between clients. Each client receives a fresh sanitary strip for the neck and we follow the Barber board law, using barbercide for combs and tools.


What if I have to cancel at the last minute?

If you know in advance you can’t make your appointment, please cancel your appointment on freshbarber.com. Cancelations made 24 hours in advance are not subject to fee. If it’s less than 24 hours away, please e-mail hello@freshbarber to let us know.
